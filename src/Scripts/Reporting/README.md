# Reporting

This folder contains ESAPI scripts for generating clinical reports and data exports.

## Purpose

Reporting scripts automate the generation of treatment summaries, dose reports, and clinical documentation.

## Typical Reports

- Treatment summary reports
- DVH analysis and export
- Dose statistics
- Plan comparison reports
- Clinical documentation
- Data export for research (anonymized)

## Data Privacy

⚠️ **IMPORTANT**: Ensure all exported data complies with HIPAA, GDPR, and local privacy regulations. Remove or anonymize patient identifiable information before sharing any reports outside your institution.
